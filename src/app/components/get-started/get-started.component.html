Briefing Buddy

An AI chatbot tool for the Government of Alberta that centralizes staff data and allows managers to ask questions about projects and initiatives.

Use this service to:

  • Get quick answers about Government of Alberta projects and initiatives
  • Access centralized staff data and information
  • Generate briefing notes and reports based on available data

Before you begin

You'll need to sign in with your Government of Alberta credentials to access the full features.

To get the most out of Briefing Buddy, you should:

  • Have specific questions about GoA projects or initiatives
  • Be prepared to provide feedback on AI-generated responses

Other information about the service

Briefing Buddy uses AI to analyze Government of Alberta staff data and documentation. It can help executives and managers quickly find information, generate summaries, and identify knowledge gaps in your organization's documentation. The system continuously improves as more information is added to the knowledge base.
